Laser Door Alarm

A DIY laser door alarm is a security device created using a laser pointer and a light sensor. It works by placing the laser pointer and the light sensor opposite each other across a doorway or entry point. When the laser beam is interrupted by someone passing through the doorway, it triggers the light sensor, which then activates an alarm or alert system. This simple yet effective setup provides a low-cost solution for monitoring entry points and alerting homeowners to potential intruders or unauthorized access. AC-DC Power Supply

An AC-DC power supply is an electronic device that converts alternating current (AC) electrical power from a mains power source into direct current (DC) power. It typically consists of a transformer, rectifier, and filtering components. The transformer steps down the voltage from the mains power source, while the rectifier converts the AC voltage into DC voltage. Filtering components smooth out the resulting DC voltage to remove any remaining fluctuations or noise. AC-DC power supplies are commonly used in various electronic devices and appliances to provide the stable and regulated DC power necessary for their operation. My project whn i was grade 9.

AC-DC TRANSFORMER

An AC-DC transformer is an electrical device that converts alternating current (AC) electrical energy from a power source into direct current (DC) electrical energy. It typically consists of a primary coil and a secondary coil wound around a core made of ferromagnetic material. When an AC voltage is applied to the primary coil, it induces a changing magnetic field in the core, which in turn induces a voltage in the secondary coil. This secondary voltage is then rectified and filtered to produce a stable DC output voltage. AC-DC transformers are commonly used in power supplies to convert mains AC voltage to the DC voltage required by electronic devices such as computers, appliances, and electronic gadgets. DIVIDING NETWORK

A dividing network, commonly known as a crossover network, is an essential component in high-quality sound speaker systems. Its purpose is to split the audio signal into different frequency bands and direct them to the appropriate speaker drivers (such as woofers, mid-range drivers, and tweeters) optimized for reproducing those frequencies. By dividing the audio signal into frequency bands, the dividing network ensures that each speaker driver operates within its most efficient frequency range, thereby enhancing the overall sound quality and fidelity of the speaker system. This prevents distortion and ensures that each driver reproduces the frequencies it's best suited for, resulting in clearer sound reproduction with improved imaging and tonal balance. Dividing networks can be passive, utilizing combinations of capacitors, inductors, and resistors to split and filter the signal, or active, employing electronic circuits and amplification to achieve similar results. The design and implementation of the dividing network play a crucial role in determining the sonic characteristics and performance of the speaker system. Also our project during Work Immersion.

SUMO BOT

A sumo bot is a small, autonomous robot designed to compete in sumo-style wrestling matches against other robots. These competitions typically take place within a ring, often resembling a traditional sumo wrestling arena. The objective of a sumo bot match is for one robot to push its opponent out of the ring or to immobilize it within a specified time frame. Sumo bots are equipped with sensors, motors, and control systems that enable them to navigate the arena, detect opponents, and execute strategies to gain the upper hand in a match. They may utilize various techniques such as aggressive pushing, evasive maneuvers, or clever tactics to outmaneuver their opponents and achieve victory. Sumo bot competitions are popular among robotics enthusiasts and provide a fun and competitive platform for showcasing engineering skills, creativity, and innovation in autonomous robot design and programming.
